--- layout: title-top title: Supply Chain Definition main: u-bg-warehouse-2 wrap: u-bs-common audio: Yes --- {{#> column col="12" class="u-bg-gray-lightest"}}

The flow of material used to create a product or set of products from the raw material providers to the end user.

  • Encompasses not just VA’s actions, but the actions of its suppliers and supplier’s suppliers.
  • Supply chain can cover both material and service related activities.
